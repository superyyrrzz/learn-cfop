Learn CFOP

Master the world's most popular speedcubing method with interactive 3D animations. Go from beginner to sub-30 seconds.

CFOP is solved in four steps, always in this order:

Step 1
C

Cross

Build a white cross on the bottom face, with each edge matching its center color. This is the foundation for everything else.
Step 2
F

First Two Layers

After the cross, pair each white corner with its matching edge and insert them into the slots around the bottom two layers.
Step 3
O

Orient Last Layer

Once F2L is done, orient all yellow stickers to face upward. First fix edges (make a yellow cross), then fix corners.
Step 4
P

Permute Last Layer

Yellow is all on top, but pieces may be in the wrong positions. Swap corners into place, then cycle edges to finish the solve.
