F2L -- First Two Layers

Pair corner and edge pieces and insert them into their slots. Start with these basic cases, then develop intuition for all 41 cases.

When do I do this?

After the white cross is solved. You now have a white cross on the bottom with all four edges matching their centers. The next goal is to fill in the four corner-edge pairs (called "slots") that complete the bottom two layers. You'll repeat this process four times -- once for each slot.

How do I recognize what to do?

Find a white corner piece in the top layer (it has white plus two other colors). Then find the matching edge piece (the edge with those same two non-white colors). Your job is to pair them together and insert them into the correct slot. Look at where the white sticker faces on the corner and where the edge is relative to the corner -- each combination below shows a different starting arrangement.

Tip

If a corner or edge is stuck in the wrong slot in the bottom two layers, do R U R' (or the mirror) to pull it out to the top layer first, then solve the pair normally.
